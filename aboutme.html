About Me

I am Simon Lucas. I was born in December of 2000 and grew up in Port Hope, Ontario.

Ever since I was a kid, I wanted to create games or movies like the ones I had.

I decided to focus on taking a career path in either animation or games.

Learned at Loyalist College's Animation Program and then at Munster Technological University in Ireland in their Animation Program.
